About Us

Who We Are

Radiko Khaitan Limited is a private-recognised skill training institution committed to empowering youth with practical, market-relevant skills.

We believe a degree is not always the path to success — a strong hunar (skill) is. Start your own business, get employed, or become self-sufficient with our courses.

All training is offline and in-person to ensure maximum learning quality under expert guidance.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Aapke common sawaalon ke jawab — free courses se lekar certificate tak sab kuch.

Course ki fees kitni hai?
▼
🎉 Bilkul FREE hai! Radiko Khaitan Limited mein saare courses muft hain — koi fees nahi, koi hidden charges nahi. Yeh ek government supported skill training center hai jahan sabko free mein hunar sikhaya jaata hai.
Kya course online bhi hota hai?
▼
Nahi, hamare saare courses 100% offline aur in-person hain. Hum believe karte hain ki practical skills sirf hands-on training se aate hain, isliye koi bhi course online nahi hai.
Certificate kitne time mein milta hai?
▼
Course completion ke baad Sanstha Certificate turant milta hai. Government Certificate verification process ke baad milta hai jisme thoda time lag sakta hai. Dono certificates ka status aap Student Portal mein dekh sakte hain.
Minimum qualification kya chahiye enrollment ke liye?
▼
Zyaadatar courses ke liye koi minimum qualification nahi hai. Koi bhi — chahe 5th pass ho ya graduate — enroll kar sakta hai. Kuch specific courses ke liye basic reading/writing jaroori hai.
Classes ka time kya hota hai?
▼
Classes Monday se Saturday, 10 AM–2 PM aur 3 PM–6 PM chalti hain. Aap apni suvidha ke hisaab se morning ya evening batch choose kar sakte hain.
Kya job placement bhi milti hai course ke baad?
▼
Hum job placement guarantee nahi dete, lekin hamara network aur career guidance zaroor milta hai. Hamare students khud businesses shuru kar rahe hain ya locally employed ho rahe hain apni skills se.
Ek student ek se zyada courses kar sakta hai?
▼
Haan! Ek course complete karne ke baad aap doosra course enroll kar sakte hain. Ek time mein ek hi course allowed hai taaki focus bana rahe.
